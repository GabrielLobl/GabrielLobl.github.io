  • Dice - Pro

    This app is the first iOS app I ever made. It's a simple app, but the satisfaction I had when I completed it was huge. The app is basically a virtual dice which you can use to play board games, casino games or just get a random number. The app has the option to roll just one die or a pair of dice. I personally use it while playing monopoly with my family, but it's great for any occasion.

  • Shape Calculator

    After submitting The Dice to the App Store, I wanted to challenge myself by making something bigger, something more complex. I learned how to perform math operations with code and I knew I had to use this knowledge in my next app. I decided to make an app that would calculate the geometrical properties of shapes by using simple formulas. For example, you could calculate the area of a circle, by just providing the radius. I worked in the app for weeks until I finally completed it. It's totally free and you can check it out here.

    Percentage Calculator is my personal favorite when it comes to design.
